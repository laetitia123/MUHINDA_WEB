    About

    About Us

    MUHINDA COMPANY LTD is headed by lean, experienced, Board of Directors [BOD] with a wide experience in multicultural environments, a Chief Executive Officer [CEO] who oversees day to day running of the company, and implementation of BOD policies and strategies. The CEO serves as a link between the BOD and other stakeholders. The CEO works with a team of functional directors charged with several responsibilities needed to keep the company running. It is these combined skills, expertise and knowledge that makes MUHINDA COMPANY LTD, one of the fastest growing service providers in the industry.

    Vission

    The consistent good performance of the business is based on its core service delivery and staff commitment. The vision is envisaged on the key elements like; supply of quality products, customer care, good working conditions, hygienic and environmental friendly atmosphere as an aid to the optimum achievement.

    Mission

    To support organizations to achieve their objectives by providing high quality products, customer care and support services in all principal areas of operation.

    KEYS TO SUCCESS

    • 01 • A qualified, experienced and skilled management and supervision team and a committed, skilled, well trained and competent team of staff;
    • 02 • Capability developed from dealing with diversified clientele;
    • 03 • Timely delivery schedules;
    • 04 • The use of good standards of quality control, especially in health and safety regulations, environment and quality materials and equipment;
    • 05 • Responsiveness and flexibility in human resource management that is made possible by innovation, supervisory effectiveness and training;
    • 06 • A concern for health and environment;
    • 07 • Total quality management in all our operations
